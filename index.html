Beautiful rose

The rose scent is one of the flower's most treasured characteristics. Greeks and Romans made perfume from roses, and to this day, their scent is an important element in many of the world's best and most successful perfume blends. Centifolia and damask roses are the most common choices used to make perfume.

Beautiful cactus

One of the most unique things about cacti is their ability to store large amounts of water in their stems. This adaptation helps them to survive in desert conditions where water is scarce.

Beautiful lily

Christianity considers the lily to be a symbol of purity and chastity. For this reason, people associate white lily flowers with the Virgin Mary. These days, husbands and wives give the lily to each other as a traditional 30th-anniversary gift. This is because lilies represent humility and devotion.
